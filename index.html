Lemonade: the Best Beverage Out There

Most Refreshing Drink

Lemonade really hits the spot on those nice, hot summer days.

Think about the this: the sun is baring down, your throat is dry, you need something to quench your thirst. Lemonade is the perfect answer!

Ice Cold Temperature

An essential step to the best lemonade is to have it ice cold. Warm lemonade just doesn't make the cut.

Steps to the Best Lemonade

These steps are a full proof plan to make the best glass of lemonade:

Get the ingredients
Having fresh ingredients, especially the lemons, is key to success
Squeeze and Grab the Seeds
Get as much juice as you can and pulp is needed. Really squeeze!
Add the Water and Sugar
Be extra generous with the sugar but don't be too sweet
